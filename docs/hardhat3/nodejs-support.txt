[40]#Node.js support

   Hardhat 3 launches with the support of [41]Node.js node --version ≥
   v22.10.0 and node --version ≥ v24.0.0.

   After launch, Hardhat 3 will support all subsequent even major versions
   of Node.js, while they are labeled as Current, Active LTS and
   Maintenance. This support activates with the launch of Node.js v26 and
   will continue thereafter.
